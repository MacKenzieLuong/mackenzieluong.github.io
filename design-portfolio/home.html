ratund

Aug. 2025
Game Design | 3D | Procreate

A chaotically fun rat-themed game created for Micro Jam 45's themes of "Miniature" and "Gambling Addiction". Players struggle (intentionally!) to control a cheese-obsessed rat to find any possible way to get more round. I designed over-the-top, cheesy UI elements and tried my hand at 3D texturing for the first time. As this game had to be completed in 48 hours, the designs were mainly low fidelity sketches created in Procreate to quickly iterate and adapt to the fast-paced development cycle.

Event Social Platform

Sep. 2024 - Dec. 2024
Android App | Figma

My first high fidelity mockup is for an Android app for event hosting and sign ins. Working collaboratively with other CS students, we developed a consistent design system inspired by popular social apps while ensuring intuitive navigation. We also wanted to keep a cute blue theme as a reference to the pokemon Marill, who was also our team name! This project was my first large step for my understanding of Figma's collaborative features and mobile design patterns.
